About Me Mahabali

Greetings! I am Mahabali,
the great king of Kerala, known for my unparalleled devotion to my people and my deep sense of justice.
My reign was marked by prosperity and harmony, where no one went hungry and all lived in peace.
However, my humility and dedication to my subjects caught the attention of the gods, leading to my temporary exile.
Each year, during the festival of Onam, I return to visit my beloved people, bringing joy and celebration.
Welcome to my realm!
